Huzzah

Instructions

The principle of the game is simple: the player has to turn 2 (two) cards at a time until an identical pair is found. The purpose of the game is to clear the deck, finding all matching pairs, in the time given.

In each round 4 new cards are added to the series and the game becomes increasingly difficult because the player's memory is more and more challenged.

Here is how to play the game:

  1. At the beginning of the game, press the Start button.
  2. You will have to turn 2 (two) cards at a time, randomly, within a set timer, until the matching cocktail cards are found.
  3. Once all the paired cocktail cards are found, you can move to the next level.
  4. In each round 4 new cards are added to the series and the game becomes all the more difficult as your memory is challenged to pair all the cards within a set timer.
  5. If you don't find all the matching cards within the set time, then you will have to start the game again!
